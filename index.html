THE World University Rankings 2021

World University Rankings vs Overall Scores

University rankings are rankings of institutions in higher education based on a variety of determining factors. There are many major international ranking websites, such as the QS World University Rankings, the Times Higher Education (THE) Rankings, the Academic Ranking of World Universities, etc. This visualization will be based off the THE Times Higher Education Rankings data for the year 2021.

This shows the top 100 universities around the world by THE rankings based on their overall scores.

Click on the legend to filter the data points by location.

University Count Map

This shows the map of universities count in different world countries.

We can see that the United States tops the list for having the most number of universities.

Student Map

This shows the map of student number in different world countries.

Student counts in United States and China emerge as the first and second places for having the highest number of university students.

Radar chart

This shows the breakdown analysis of top 3 unis in 2021 by THE.

We can see that these 3 universities differ most by their industry income scores.
