THE World University Rankings 2021

University rankings are rankings of institutions in higher education based on a variety of determining factors. This visualization will be based off the Times Higher Education (THE) Rankings data for the year 2021. The Times Higher Education (THE) World University Rankings is one of the largest and most diverse university rankings to date, as for the year 2021 alone, the ranking includes more than 1500 universities across 93 countries and regions.

Universities Are Ranked By Overall Scores

The Times Higher Education Ranking (THE) system ranks universities worldwide based on a few important areas, which are teaching, research, knowledge transfer and international outlook. These performance indicators will then be calculated give an average overall score, in which gives us the rankings of the universities worldwide.
